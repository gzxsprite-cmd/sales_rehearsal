# 00 Project Positioning

## Product name

Sales Rehearsal AI

## What it is

This is a sales conversation rehearsal product.

It is designed to help sales rehearse key customer conversations before real meetings, instead of only reading materials or attending training.

## What problem it solves

In VMS product promotion, the real problem is often not that the product is undefined, but that sales are not ready at the actual conversation level.

Typical gaps:
- sales know the product, but do not know how to explain it to a customer
- sales know some selling points, but do not connect them to the customer’s real concerns
- sales do not discover knowledge gaps until the real customer conversation happens
- existing enterprise knowledge exists, but is not activated at the moment of conversation

## Core idea

Shift preparation from:

read materials → attend training → go to customer

to:

rehearse → expose gaps → review → push missing knowledge → rehearse again

## Why start with VMS

VMS is the right entry point because:
- it is new
- sales are still learning how to sell it
- software selling logic is different from traditional hardware/BOM logic
- it has real promotion campaigns and real business timing

## What this repo is for

This repo is not for a full product build.

This repo is for:
- concept validation
- page/interaction validation
- stakeholder demo
- Codex-guided demo generation
